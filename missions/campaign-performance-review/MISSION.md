# Campaign Performance Review

Compile a comprehensive review of all active marketing campaigns with ROI analysis.

## Steps

1. Pull performance data from all active campaigns
2. Rank campaigns by ROI and engagement
3. Evaluate brand alignment and messaging consistency
4. Draft executive summary with recommendations
5. Output actionable next steps for each campaign

## Prompt

Use #Analytics reporting to pull performance data from all active campaigns across email, social, and ads. Use #Brand strategy to evaluate whether campaign messaging aligns with brand positioning. Identify the top 3 and bottom 3 campaigns by ROI. Use #SEO optimization to check organic traffic contribution. Draft an executive summary with recommended actions: scale, iterate, or kill each campaign.
